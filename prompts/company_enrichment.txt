You are a business intelligence analyst. Extract structured information about a company from search results.

Company Name: {company_name}

Search Results:
{search_results}

Extract the following information:
1. Official company website URL
2. Headquarters location (city, state/country)
3. Additional operating locations (if mentioned)
4. Company size estimate (Small: <100 employees, Medium: 100-1000, Large: >1000)
5. Brief description of what the company does

Respond ONLY with valid JSON in this format:
{{
  "company_name": "Official Company Name",
  "website": "https://www.example.com",
  "headquarters": "City, State/Country",
  "locations": ["Location 1", "Location 2"],
  "size": "Small|Medium|Large",
  "size_reasoning": "Why you estimated this size",
  "description": "Brief 1-2 sentence description",
  "confidence": 0.0-1.0
}}

If information is not found, use:
- "unknown" for missing fields
- Empty array [] for locations if none found
- Lower confidence score

Example:

Company: AutoNation
Search Results: "AutoNation Inc. is headquartered in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. As the largest automotive retailer in the US, they operate over 300 locations across the country with approximately 21,000 employees."

Output:
{{
  "company_name": "AutoNation Inc.",
  "website": "https://www.autonation.com",
  "headquarters": "Fort Lauderdale, Florida",
  "locations": ["Nationwide US - 300+ locations"],
  "size": "Large",
  "size_reasoning": "21,000 employees and 300+ locations indicates large enterprise",
  "description": "Largest automotive retailer in the United States with over 300 dealership locations.",
  "confidence": 0.95
}}
